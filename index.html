The Road to Summer 2027

It was a sweltering night in Toronto, summer 2025. The air was thick, and I said the words that would change everything: "I need some throat lube."

Twenty years in Toronto hospitality taught me that the best nights start with a laugh and end with genuine connection. That's Throat Hydration.

We're a premium 5% craft lager with a name that makes people smile and values we take dead seriously. The name is playful, but the commitment? That's the realest part. With rainbow lips on every can—not just in June—we are Toronto-born, not a corporate import.

Enjoy the beer, enjoy the laughs. Cheers, to many more.
